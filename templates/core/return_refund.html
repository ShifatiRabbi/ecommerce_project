Our Return & Refund Policy

At {{ site_settings.site_name }}, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for returns and refunds.

30-Day Return Policy: Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Process

1
Initiate Return

Contact our customer service team within 30 days of receiving your order to initiate the return process. You can reach us at {{ site_settings.contact_email }} or {{ site_settings.contact_phone }}.

2
Package Item

Pack the item in its original packaging with all tags and accessories included. Include the original invoice and reason for return.

3
Ship Item

We'll provide you with a return shipping label or instructions on where to ship the item. For cash on delivery orders, you may need to visit our nearest drop-off point.

4
Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund within 7-10 business days. You'll receive confirmation via email.

Refund Timeline

Day 1-2

We receive and inspect your returned item

Day 3-5

Refund is processed and approved

Day 6-10

Refund appears in your account (depending on payment method)

Eligible for Return

Eligible Items
  • Unused products
  • Original packaging
  • All tags attached
  • Within 30 days
  • Defective products
Non-Returnable Items
  • Personalized items
  • Perishable goods
  • Intimate products
  • Digital products
  • Used or damaged items

Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for items in different sizes or colors, subject to availability. The exchange process follows the same timeline as returns.

Note: If the item you want to exchange is out of stock, we'll issue a refund instead.

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

Please provide photos of the damaged item and packaging to help us process your claim faster.
